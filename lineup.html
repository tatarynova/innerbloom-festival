Featured artists

  • Artbat

    Genre: techno/house/electronic-dance

    Origin: Kyiv, Ukraine

    Biography: A DJ/producer duo from Kyiv (Artur Kryvenko & Vitaliy Limarenko), formed in 2014. They broke through quickly after their track “Mandrake” and gained support from major DJs. Over time they rose to global prominence with chart-topping releases, festival appearances, and — in 2021 — launching their own label. Their evolving sound and energetic performances have made them a major act in contemporary electronic dance music.

  • Boris Brejcha

    Genre: minimal techno

    Origin: Germany

    Biography: German DJ producer, born 1981. He began producing in his teens, and in 2006 released his first tracks and played his first festival. He created the “High-Tech Minimal” style, founded his own label, and — with his trademark Joker mask — became a major force on the global techno/festival circuit.

  • Monolink

    Genre: ambient/minimal techno/house

    Origin: Germany

    Biography: Real name Steffen Linck, a German singer-songwriter turned electronic producer. Monolink merges live vocals, guitar/piano and electronic beats — offering emotionally rich, atmospheric sets. He released his debut album in 2018 and has since grown into one of the defining voices bridging live instrumentation and dancefloor-oriented electronica.

  • Rufus Du Sol

    Genre: Indie dance/deep house

    Origin: Australia

    Biography: Australian trio from Sydney (formed 2010). They gained recognition for their atmospheric, emotionally charged blend of electronic production and live instrumentation. Over years they have released multiple albums and toured globally, becoming one of the most successful electronic-drawing acts from Australia, known for lush vocals, synth-driven grooves and immersive live shows.

  • Jan Blomqvist

    Genre: melodic house/deep house

    Origin: Germany

    Biography: German singer, songwriter, producer and live performer — originally in rock/grunge-type bands. After moving to Berlin, he pivoted to electronic music, combining his vocal and instrumental roots with club-ready electronic production. Known for his emotionally melodic, introspective sound and live shows that blend songcraft with dancefloor energy. His 2025 album “MUTE” reflects his evolving artistry.

  • Lane 8

    Genre: melodic house/deep house

    Origin: USA

    Biography: Daniel Goldstein, known professionally as Lane 8, is an American DJ/producer who rose to prominence on Anjunadeep with his 2015 debut Rise and later founded the This Never Happened label and show concept that bans phone recording to encourage presence. He’s known for emotive, journey-style productions and large outdoor gatherings, and has released multiple studio albums including Little by Little (2018), Brightest Lights (2020), Reviver (2022) and Childish (2024).
